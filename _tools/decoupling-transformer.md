The Decoupling Transformer reformulates a classical planning task so that the
benefits of star-topology decoupled search become available to any standard
planner, without modifying the planner itself. The approach and its empirical
evaluation are described in our ICAPS 2024 paper {% cite speck-gnad-icaps2024 %}.

The implementation is available on
[GitHub](https://github.com/speckdavid/decoupling-transformer).

See also [Decoupling Transformer SAT](/tools/decoupling-transformer-sat/),
which extends this work with SAT-based planning.
