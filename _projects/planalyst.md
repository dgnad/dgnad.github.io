Planalyst analyzes PDDL planning problems by counting how many plans a task
admits, using methods such as model counting, decision-DNNF compilation, and
plan enumeration. It integrates several planning and reasoning tools to support
counting and reasoning with planning solutions, as introduced in our AAAI 2025
paper {% cite speck-et-al-aaai2025 %}.

The implementation is available on
[GitHub](https://github.com/speckdavid/planalyst).

See also the related [PlanPilot](/projects/planpilot/) project for interactive
exploration of plan spaces.
